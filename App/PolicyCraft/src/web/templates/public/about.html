About PolicyCraft

Strategic and Ethical Integration of Generative AI in Higher Education

Project Overview

PolicyCraft is a comprehensive web-based application designed to analyse university AI policies, extract key themes, classify institutional approaches, and generate strategic recommendations for higher education institutions navigating the integration of generative AI.

Key Features

  • Document Analysis: Upload and process policy documents in multiple formats
  • Theme Extraction: Automatically identify key policy themes using NLP
  • Policy Classification: Classify approaches as restrictive, permissive, or balanced
  • Visual Analytics: Interactive charts and dashboards for insights
  • Recommendations: Generate tailored policy suggestions
  • Secure Access: User-specific data separation and privacy protection
